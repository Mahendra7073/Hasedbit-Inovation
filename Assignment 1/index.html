What is Robotics?

Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, creation, operation, and use of robots.

Robotics in Daily Life

Robotics is used in cleaning robots, robotic arms in factories, robotic surgeries, self-driving cars, and even in toys.

Future of Robotics

The future of robotics includes human-like robots, AI-powered machines, robots in space missions, and automation in every industry.

Robotics Ethics

Robotics ethics ensure robots are used safely, do not harm humans, protect privacy, and are used only for good purposes.

Additional Info

Robotics combines fields like mechanical engineering, electronics, and computer science to create smart machines that work automatically.
